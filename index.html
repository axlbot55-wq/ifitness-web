How It Works

A structured onboarding process ensures your program is safe, personalized, and set up for results from day one.

01

Pre-Screening & PAR-Q

Before training begins, every client completes a Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire (PAR-Q) to identify any health conditions and ensure your program is built safely and precisely for your body.

02

Initial Consultation

A 30-minute session — in person or via video call — to understand your goals, lifestyle, and training history. We build your customized plan from the ground up.

03

Baseline Measurements & Goal Setting

We establish your starting point across key metrics:
